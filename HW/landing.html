Summary: Latitude vs X

Whether financial, political, or social -- data's true power lies in its ability to answer questions definitively. This site will help answer a fundamental question: "What's the weather like as we approach the equator?"

To accomplish this, I utilized a simple Python library, the OpenWeatherMap API, a Python script and a little common sense to create a representative model of weather across world cities.

I select at least 500 unique (non-repeat) cities based on latitude and longitude geocoordinates to give me a better representation of weather around the world and showcase the following relationships:


Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
